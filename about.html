YHWH Tech provides financial consulting services for Forex and cryptocurrency firms, traders & Experts. We make available for our customers, extremely accurate data on specific currency pairs and coins. The system of currency pair analysis is based on the combination of highly sophisticated mathematical techniques namely; Fourier series, First Order Ordinary Differential Equations, Newton-Raphson iteration, probability distribution as well some principles and theories applied from statistical and quantum mechanics.

This method absolutely constrains the discrepancies and uncertainties in the foreign exchange market such that the market's probabilistic effects become somewhat "critically damped" making it extremely negligible. This approach is phenomenal and greatly distinguished from all the available traditional methods of signal evaluation. Due to its sensitive and precise nature, it is only Limited only to pairs whose value lie in the range of the statistical table of The Probabilistic Distribution function; The Normal Distribution Function.
